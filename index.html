About

Welcome to Pro-Co Solutions, your new landscaping provider servicing central Oklahoma. We take pride in transforming outdoor spaces into beautiful, well-maintained havens. Whether you need a simple lawn cleanup or a complete garden overhaul, we've got you covered.

Services

  • Lawn Cleanups

    Fall Cleanup: As the leaves start to fall, our team will tidy up your lawn, remove debris, and prepare it for winter.

    Spring Cleanup: Let us rejuvenate your lawn after the cold season. We'll clear away dead leaves, branches, and other clutter, ensuring a fresh start for your landscape.

  • Mowing and Edging

    Precision Mowing: Our skilled crew will keep your grass at the perfect height, promoting healthy growth and a manicured appearance.

    Edging: Clean, crisp edges around flower beds, walkways, and driveways enhance the overall aesthetics of your property.

  • Weed Removal

    Weed Control: Say goodbye to pesky weeds! We'll tackle them efficiently, preventing them from taking over your garden beds and lawns.

    Mulching: Our organic mulch not only suppresses weeds but also enriches the soil and retains moisture.

  • Garden Care

    Planting and Design: From colorful annuals to perennial favorites, we'll create a stunning garden that complements your home.

    Pruning and Trimming: Our experts will shape your shrubs, maintain their health, and ensure they thrive year-round.

  • Tree and Shrub Care

    Tree Pruning: Proper pruning enhances tree structure, promotes growth, and minimizes safety risks.

    Shrub Care: We'll nurture your shrubs, keeping them healthy, disease-free, and visually appealing.

  • Bush Removals

    Overgrown Bushes: If your bushes have outgrown their space, we'll safely remove them, leaving room for new plantings.

    Selective Clearing: Our team will strategically remove unwanted bushes while preserving the ones you love.

  • Planting

    Seasonal Plantings: Whether it's spring bulbs, summer annuals, or fall mums, we'll plant the right species at the right time.

    Tree Planting: Enhance your landscape with shade trees, ornamentals, or fruit-bearing trees.
